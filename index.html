What is "Freaky"?

Freaky is when things go from zero to 'what the heck’ real quick. It's that vibe when you're chilling, but suddenly your playlist jumps from a TikTok dance bop to creepy elevator music. Imagine your weird cousin who’s a little too obsessed with conspiracy theories, or that one friend who texts you 'we need to talk’ at 2 a.m. It’s giving spooky but with a side of chaotic energy—like an offbeat mix of cringe and 'I kinda like it.’ Freaky is basically life’s glitch in the matrix, and you’re just here for the ride.
